This document is a quick summary of the distribution of nitrate data collected by the Environmental Protection Commission of Hillsborough County. Source data are available here. Nitrates and nitrites have been collectively measured since the early 1980s, with only nitrate quantified prior.

Only data for the last ten years was evaluated below to describe the relative distribution of nitrate/nitrite by site type (freshwater or marine) or the designated use.
